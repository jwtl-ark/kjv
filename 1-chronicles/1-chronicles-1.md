# 1 Chronicles 1

**1** Adam, Sheth, Enosh, ^1-chronicles-1-1

**2** Kenan, Mahalaleel, Jered, ^1-chronicles-1-2

**3** Henoch, Methuselah, Lamech, ^1-chronicles-1-3

**4** Noah, Shem, Ham, and Japheth. ^1-chronicles-1-4

**5** The sons of Japheth; Gomer, and Magog, and Madai, and Javan, and Tubal, and Meshech, and Tiras. ^1-chronicles-1-5

**6** And the sons of Gomer; Ashchenaz, and Riphath, and Togarmah. ^1-chronicles-1-6

**7** And the sons of Javan; Elishah, and Tarshish, Kittim, and Dodanim. ^1-chronicles-1-7

**8** The sons of Ham; Cush, and Mizraim, Put, and Canaan. ^1-chronicles-1-8

**9** And the sons of Cush; Seba, and Havilah, and Sabta, and Raamah, and Sabtecha. And the sons of Raamah; Sheba, and Dedan. ^1-chronicles-1-9

**10** And Cush begat Nimrod: he began to be mighty upon the earth. ^1-chronicles-1-10

**11** And Mizraim begat Ludim, and Anamim, and Lehabim, and Naphtuhim, ^1-chronicles-1-11

**12** And Pathrusim, and Casluhim, (of whom came the Philistines,) and Caphthorim. ^1-chronicles-1-12

**13** And Canaan begat Zidon his firstborn, and Heth, ^1-chronicles-1-13

**14** The Jebusite also, and the Amorite, and the Girgashite, ^1-chronicles-1-14

**15** And the Hivite, and the Arkite, and the Sinite, ^1-chronicles-1-15

**16** And the Arvadite, and the Zemarite, and the Hamathite. ^1-chronicles-1-16

**17** The sons of Shem; Elam, and Asshur, and Arphaxad, and Lud, and Aram, and Uz, and Hul, and Gether, and Meshech. ^1-chronicles-1-17

**18** And Arphaxad begat Shelah, and Shelah begat Eber. ^1-chronicles-1-18

**19** And unto Eber were born two sons: the name of the one was Peleg; because in his days the earth was divided: and his brother’s name was Joktan. ^1-chronicles-1-19

**20** And Joktan begat Almodad, and Sheleph, and Hazarmaveth, and Jerah, ^1-chronicles-1-20

**21** Hadoram also, and Uzal, and Diklah, ^1-chronicles-1-21

**22** And Ebal, and Abimael, and Sheba, ^1-chronicles-1-22

**23** And Ophir, and Havilah, and Jobab. All these were the sons of Joktan. ^1-chronicles-1-23

**24** Shem, Arphaxad, Shelah, ^1-chronicles-1-24

**25** Eber, Peleg, Reu, ^1-chronicles-1-25

**26** Serug, Nahor, Terah, ^1-chronicles-1-26

**27** Abram; the same is Abraham. ^1-chronicles-1-27

**28** The sons of Abraham; Isaac, and Ishmael. ^1-chronicles-1-28

**29** These are their generations: The firstborn of Ishmael, Nebaioth; then Kedar, and Adbeel, and Mibsam, ^1-chronicles-1-29

**30** Mishma, and Dumah, Massa, Hadad, and Tema, ^1-chronicles-1-30

**31** Jetur, Naphish, and Kedemah. These are the sons of Ishmael. ^1-chronicles-1-31

**32** Now the sons of Keturah, Abraham’s concubine: she bare Zimran, and Jokshan, and Medan, and Midian, and Ishbak, and Shuah. And the sons of Jokshan; Sheba, and Dedan. ^1-chronicles-1-32

**33** And the sons of Midian; Ephah, and Epher, and Henoch, and Abida, and Eldaah. All these are the sons of Keturah. ^1-chronicles-1-33

**34** And Abraham begat Isaac. The sons of Isaac; Esau and Israel. ^1-chronicles-1-34

**35** The sons of Esau; Eliphaz, Reuel, and Jeush, and Jaalam, and Korah. ^1-chronicles-1-35

**36** The sons of Eliphaz; Teman, and Omar, Zephi, and Gatam, Kenaz, and Timna, and Amalek. ^1-chronicles-1-36

**37** The sons of Reuel; Nahath, Zerah, Shammah, and Mizzah. ^1-chronicles-1-37

**38** And the sons of Seir; Lotan, and Shobal, and Zibeon, and Anah, and Dishon, and Ezar, and Dishan. ^1-chronicles-1-38

**39** And the sons of Lotan; Hori, and Homam: and Timna was Lotan’s sister. ^1-chronicles-1-39

**40** The sons of Shobal; Alian, and Manahath, and Ebal, Shephi, and Onam. And the sons of Zibeon; Aiah, and Anah. ^1-chronicles-1-40

**41** The sons of Anah; Dishon. And the sons of Dishon; Amram, and Eshban, and Ithran, and Cheran. ^1-chronicles-1-41

**42** The sons of Ezer; Bilhan, and Zavan, and Jakan. The sons of Dishan; Uz, and Aran. ^1-chronicles-1-42

**43** Now these are the kings that reigned in the land of Edom before any king reigned over the children of Israel; Bela the son of Beor: and the name of his city was Dinhabah. ^1-chronicles-1-43

**44** And when Bela was dead, Jobab the son of Zerah of Bozrah reigned in his stead. ^1-chronicles-1-44

**45** And when Jobab was dead, Husham of the land of the Temanites reigned in his stead. ^1-chronicles-1-45

**46** And when Husham was dead, Hadad the son of Bedad, which smote Midian in the field of Moab, reigned in his stead: and the name of his city was Avith. ^1-chronicles-1-46

**47** And when Hadad was dead, Samlah of Masrekah reigned in his stead. ^1-chronicles-1-47

**48** And when Samlah was dead, Shaul of Rehoboth by the river reigned in his stead. ^1-chronicles-1-48

**49** And when Shaul was dead, Baalhanan the son of Achbor reigned in his stead. ^1-chronicles-1-49

**50** And when Baalhanan was dead, Hadad reigned in his stead: and the name of his city was Pai; and his wife’s name was Mehetabel, the daughter of Matred, the daughter of Mezahab. ^1-chronicles-1-50

**51** Hadad died also. And the dukes of Edom were; duke Timnah, duke Aliah, duke Jetheth, ^1-chronicles-1-51

**52** Duke Aholibamah, duke Elah, duke Pinon, ^1-chronicles-1-52

**53** Duke Kenaz, duke Teman, duke Mibzar, ^1-chronicles-1-53

**54** Duke Magdiel, duke Iram. These are the dukes of Edom. ^1-chronicles-1-54
