# 1 Chronicles 8

**1** Now Benjamin begat Bela his firstborn, Ashbel the second, and Aharah the third, ^1-chronicles-8-1

**2** Nohah the fourth, and Rapha the fifth. ^1-chronicles-8-2

**3** And the sons of Bela were, Addar, and Gera, and Abihud, ^1-chronicles-8-3

**4** And Abishua, and Naaman, and Ahoah, ^1-chronicles-8-4

**5** And Gera, and Shephuphan, and Huram. ^1-chronicles-8-5

**6** And these are the sons of Ehud: these are the heads of the fathers of the inhabitants of Geba, and they removed them to Manahath: ^1-chronicles-8-6

**7** And Naaman, and Ahiah, and Gera, he removed them, and begat Uzza, and Ahihud. ^1-chronicles-8-7

**8** And Shaharaim begat children in the country of Moab, after he had sent them away; Hushim and Baara were his wives. ^1-chronicles-8-8

**9** And he begat of Hodesh his wife, Jobab, and Zibia, and Mesha, and Malcham, ^1-chronicles-8-9

**10** And Jeuz, and Shachia, and Mirma. These were his sons, heads of the fathers. ^1-chronicles-8-10

**11** And of Hushim he begat Abitub, and Elpaal. ^1-chronicles-8-11

**12** The sons of Elpaal; Eber, and Misham, and Shamed, who built Ono, and Lod, with the towns thereof: ^1-chronicles-8-12

**13** Beriah also, and Shema, who were heads of the fathers of the inhabitants of Aijalon, who drove away the inhabitants of Gath: ^1-chronicles-8-13

**14** And Ahio, Shashak, and Jeremoth, ^1-chronicles-8-14

**15** And Zebadiah, and Arad, and Ader, ^1-chronicles-8-15

**16** And Michael, and Ispah, and Joha, the sons of Beriah; ^1-chronicles-8-16

**17** And Zebadiah, and Meshullam, and Hezeki, and Heber, ^1-chronicles-8-17

**18** Ishmerai also, and Jezliah, and Jobab, the sons of Elpaal; ^1-chronicles-8-18

**19** And Jakim, and Zichri, and Zabdi, ^1-chronicles-8-19

**20** And Elienai, and Zilthai, and Eliel, ^1-chronicles-8-20

**21** And Adaiah, and Beraiah, and Shimrath, the sons of Shimhi; ^1-chronicles-8-21

**22** And Ishpan, and Heber, and Eliel, ^1-chronicles-8-22

**23** And Abdon, and Zichri, and Hanan, ^1-chronicles-8-23

**24** And Hananiah, and Elam, and Antothijah, ^1-chronicles-8-24

**25** And Iphedeiah, and Penuel, the sons of Shashak; ^1-chronicles-8-25

**26** And Shamsherai, and Shehariah, and Athaliah, ^1-chronicles-8-26

**27** And Jaresiah, and Eliah, and Zichri, the sons of Jeroham. ^1-chronicles-8-27

**28** These were heads of the fathers, by their generations, chief men. These dwelt in Jerusalem. ^1-chronicles-8-28

**29** And at Gibeon dwelt the father of Gibeon; whose wife’s name was Maachah: ^1-chronicles-8-29

**30** And his firstborn son Abdon, and Zur, and Kish, and Baal, and Nadab, ^1-chronicles-8-30

**31** And Gedor, and Ahio, and Zacher. ^1-chronicles-8-31

**32** And Mikloth begat Shimeah. And these also dwelt with their brethren in Jerusalem, over against them. ^1-chronicles-8-32

**33** And Ner begat Kish, and Kish begat Saul, and Saul begat Jonathan, and Malchishua, and Abinadab, and Eshbaal. ^1-chronicles-8-33

**34** And the son of Jonathan was Meribbaal; and Meribbaal begat Micah. ^1-chronicles-8-34

**35** And the sons of Micah were, Pithon, and Melech, and Tarea, and Ahaz. ^1-chronicles-8-35

**36** And Ahaz begat Jehoadah; and Jehoadah begat Alemeth, and Azmaveth, and Zimri; and Zimri begat Moza, ^1-chronicles-8-36

**37** And Moza begat Binea: Rapha was his son, Eleasah his son, Azel his son: ^1-chronicles-8-37

**38** And Azel had six sons, whose names are these, Azrikam, Bocheru, and Ishmael, and Sheariah, and Obadiah, and Hanan. All these were the sons of Azel. ^1-chronicles-8-38

**39** And the sons of Eshek his brother were, Ulam his firstborn, Jehush the second, and Eliphelet the third. ^1-chronicles-8-39

**40** And the sons of Ulam were mighty men of valour, archers, and had many sons, and sons’ sons, an hundred and fifty. All these are of the sons of Benjamin. ^1-chronicles-8-40
