# Psalms 148

**1** Praise ye the LORD. Praise ye the LORD from the heavens: praise him in the heights. ^psalms-148-1

**2** Praise ye him, all his angels: praise ye him, all his hosts. ^psalms-148-2

**3** Praise ye him, sun and moon: praise him, all ye stars of light. ^psalms-148-3

**4** Praise him, ye heavens of heavens, and ye waters that be above the heavens. ^psalms-148-4

**5** Let them praise the name of the LORD: for he commanded, and they were created. ^psalms-148-5

**6** He hath also stablished them for ever and ever: he hath made a decree which shall not pass. ^psalms-148-6

**7** Praise the LORD from the earth, ye dragons, and all deeps: ^psalms-148-7

**8** Fire, and hail; snow, and vapours; stormy wind fulfilling his word: ^psalms-148-8

**9** Mountains, and all hills; fruitful trees, and all cedars: ^psalms-148-9

**10** Beasts, and all cattle; creeping things, and flying fowl: ^psalms-148-10

**11** Kings of the earth, and all people; princes, and all judges of the earth: ^psalms-148-11

**12** Both young men, and maidens; old men, and children: ^psalms-148-12

**13** Let them praise the name of the LORD: for his name alone is excellent; his glory is above the earth and heaven. ^psalms-148-13

**14** He also exalteth the horn of his people, the praise of all his saints; even of the children of Israel, a people near unto him. Praise ye the LORD. ^psalms-148-14
