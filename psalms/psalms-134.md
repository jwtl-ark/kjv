# Psalms 134

**1** Behold, bless ye the LORD, all ye servants of the LORD, which by night stand in the house of the LORD. ^psalms-134-1

**2** Lift up your hands in the sanctuary, and bless the LORD. ^psalms-134-2

**3** The LORD that made heaven and earth bless thee out of Zion. ^psalms-134-3
