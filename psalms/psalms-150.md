# Psalms 150

**1** Praise ye the LORD. Praise God in his sanctuary: praise him in the firmament of his power. ^psalms-150-1

**2** Praise him for his mighty acts: praise him according to his excellent greatness. ^psalms-150-2

**3** Praise him with the sound of the trumpet: praise him with the psaltery and harp. ^psalms-150-3

**4** Praise him with the timbrel and dance: praise him with stringed instruments and organs. ^psalms-150-4

**5** Praise him upon the loud cymbals: praise him upon the high sounding cymbals. ^psalms-150-5

**6** Let every thing that hath breath praise the LORD. Praise ye the LORD. ^psalms-150-6
