# Psalms 97

**1** The LORD reigneth; let the earth rejoice; let the multitude of isles be glad thereof. ^psalms-97-1

**2** Clouds and darkness are round about him: righteousness and judgment are the habitation of his throne. ^psalms-97-2

**3** A fire goeth before him, and burneth up his enemies round about. ^psalms-97-3

**4** His lightnings enlightened the world: the earth saw, and trembled. ^psalms-97-4

**5** The hills melted like wax at the presence of the LORD, at the presence of the Lord of the whole earth. ^psalms-97-5

**6** The heavens declare his righteousness, and all the people see his glory. ^psalms-97-6

**7** Confounded be all they that serve graven images, that boast themselves of idols: worship him, all ye gods. ^psalms-97-7

**8** Zion heard, and was glad; and the daughters of Judah rejoiced because of thy judgments, O LORD. ^psalms-97-8

**9** For thou, LORD, art high above all the earth: thou art exalted far above all gods. ^psalms-97-9

**10** Ye that love the LORD, hate evil: he preserveth the souls of his saints; he delivereth them out of the hand of the wicked. ^psalms-97-10

**11** Light is sown for the righteous, and gladness for the upright in heart. ^psalms-97-11

**12** Rejoice in the LORD, ye righteous; and give thanks at the remembrance of his holiness. ^psalms-97-12
