# Psalms 67

**1** God be merciful unto us, and bless us; and cause his face to shine upon us; Selah. ^psalms-67-1

**2** That thy way may be known upon earth, thy saving health among all nations. ^psalms-67-2

**3** Let the people praise thee, O God; let all the people praise thee. ^psalms-67-3

**4** O let the nations be glad and sing for joy: for thou shalt judge the people righteously, and govern the nations upon earth. Selah. ^psalms-67-4

**5** Let the people praise thee, O God; let all the people praise thee. ^psalms-67-5

**6** Then shall the earth yield her increase; and God, even our own God, shall bless us. ^psalms-67-6

**7** God shall bless us; and all the ends of the earth shall fear him. ^psalms-67-7
