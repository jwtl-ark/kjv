# Psalms 110

**1** The LORD said unto my Lord, Sit thou at my right hand, until I make thine enemies thy footstool. ^psalms-110-1

**2** The LORD shall send the rod of thy strength out of Zion: rule thou in the midst of thine enemies. ^psalms-110-2

**3** Thy people shall be willing in the day of thy power, in the beauties of holiness from the womb of the morning: thou hast the dew of thy youth. ^psalms-110-3

**4** The LORD hath sworn, and will not repent, Thou art a priest for ever after the order of Melchizedek. ^psalms-110-4

**5** The Lord at thy right hand shall strike through kings in the day of his wrath. ^psalms-110-5

**6** He shall judge among the heathen, he shall fill the places with the dead bodies; he shall wound the heads over many countries. ^psalms-110-6

**7** He shall drink of the brook in the way: therefore shall he lift up the head. ^psalms-110-7
