# Psalms 145

**1** I will extol thee, my God, O king; and I will bless thy name for ever and ever. ^psalms-145-1

**2** Every day will I bless thee; and I will praise thy name for ever and ever. ^psalms-145-2

**3** Great is the LORD, and greatly to be praised; and his greatness is unsearchable. ^psalms-145-3

**4** One generation shall praise thy works to another, and shall declare thy mighty acts. ^psalms-145-4

**5** I will speak of the glorious honour of thy majesty, and of thy wondrous works. ^psalms-145-5

**6** And men shall speak of the might of thy terrible acts: and I will declare thy greatness. ^psalms-145-6

**7** They shall abundantly utter the memory of thy great goodness, and shall sing of thy righteousness. ^psalms-145-7

**8** The LORD is gracious, and full of compassion; slow to anger, and of great mercy. ^psalms-145-8

**9** The LORD is good to all: and his tender mercies are over all his works. ^psalms-145-9

**10** All thy works shall praise thee, O LORD; and thy saints shall bless thee. ^psalms-145-10

**11** They shall speak of the glory of thy kingdom, and talk of thy power; ^psalms-145-11

**12** To make known to the sons of men his mighty acts, and the glorious majesty of his kingdom. ^psalms-145-12

**13** Thy kingdom is an everlasting kingdom, and thy dominion endureth throughout all generations. ^psalms-145-13

**14** The LORD upholdeth all that fall, and raiseth up all those that be bowed down. ^psalms-145-14

**15** The eyes of all wait upon thee; and thou givest them their meat in due season. ^psalms-145-15

**16** Thou openest thine hand, and satisfiest the desire of every living thing. ^psalms-145-16

**17** The LORD is righteous in all his ways, and holy in all his works. ^psalms-145-17

**18** The LORD is nigh unto all them that call upon him, to all that call upon him in truth. ^psalms-145-18

**19** He will fulfil the desire of them that fear him: he also will hear their cry, and will save them. ^psalms-145-19

**20** The LORD preserveth all them that love him: but all the wicked will he destroy. ^psalms-145-20

**21** My mouth shall speak the praise of the LORD: and let all flesh bless his holy name for ever and ever. ^psalms-145-21
