# Psalms 116

**1** I love the LORD, because he hath heard my voice and my supplications. ^psalms-116-1

**2** Because he hath inclined his ear unto me, therefore will I call upon him as long as I live. ^psalms-116-2

**3** The sorrows of death compassed me, and the pains of hell gat hold upon me: I found trouble and sorrow. ^psalms-116-3

**4** Then called I upon the name of the LORD; O LORD, I beseech thee, deliver my soul. ^psalms-116-4

**5** Gracious is the LORD, and righteous; yea, our God is merciful. ^psalms-116-5

**6** The LORD preserveth the simple: I was brought low, and he helped me. ^psalms-116-6

**7** Return unto thy rest, O my soul; for the LORD hath dealt bountifully with thee. ^psalms-116-7

**8** For thou hast delivered my soul from death, mine eyes from tears, and my feet from falling. ^psalms-116-8

**9** I will walk before the LORD in the land of the living. ^psalms-116-9

**10** I believed, therefore have I spoken: I was greatly afflicted: ^psalms-116-10

**11** I said in my haste, All men are liars. ^psalms-116-11

**12** What shall I render unto the LORD for all his benefits toward me? ^psalms-116-12

**13** I will take the cup of salvation, and call upon the name of the LORD. ^psalms-116-13

**14** I will pay my vows unto the LORD now in the presence of all his people. ^psalms-116-14

**15** Precious in the sight of the LORD is the death of his saints. ^psalms-116-15

**16** O LORD, truly I am thy servant; I am thy servant, and the son of thine handmaid: thou hast loosed my bonds. ^psalms-116-16

**17** I will offer to thee the sacrifice of thanksgiving, and will call upon the name of the LORD. ^psalms-116-17
