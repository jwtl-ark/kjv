# Psalms 111

**1** Praise ye the LORD. I will praise the LORD with my whole heart, in the assembly of the upright, and in the congregation. ^psalms-111-1

**2** The works of the LORD are great, sought out of all them that have pleasure therein. ^psalms-111-2

**3** His work is honourable and glorious: and his righteousness endureth for ever. ^psalms-111-3

**4** He hath made his wonderful works to be remembered: the LORD is gracious and full of compassion. ^psalms-111-4

**5** He hath given meat unto them that fear him: he will ever be mindful of his covenant. ^psalms-111-5

**6** He hath shewed his people the power of his works, that he may give them the heritage of the heathen. ^psalms-111-6

**7** The works of his hands are verity and judgment; all his commandments are sure. ^psalms-111-7

**8** They stand fast for ever and ever, and are done in truth and uprightness. ^psalms-111-8

**9** He sent redemption unto his people: he hath commanded his covenant for ever: holy and reverend is his name. ^psalms-111-9

**10** The fear of the LORD is the beginning of wisdom: a good understanding have all they that do his commandments: his praise endureth for ever. ^psalms-111-10
