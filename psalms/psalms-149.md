# Psalms 149

**1** Praise ye the LORD. Sing unto the LORD a new song, and his praise in the congregation of saints. ^psalms-149-1

**2** Let Israel rejoice in him that made him: let the children of Zion be joyful in their King. ^psalms-149-2

**3** Let them praise his name in the dance: let them sing praises unto him with the timbrel and harp. ^psalms-149-3

**4** For the LORD taketh pleasure in his people: he will beautify the meek with salvation. ^psalms-149-4

**5** Let the saints be joyful in glory: let them sing aloud upon their beds. ^psalms-149-5

**6** Let the high praises of God be in their mouth, and a two-edged sword in their hand; ^psalms-149-6

**7** To execute vengeance upon the heathen, and punishments upon the people; ^psalms-149-7

**8** To bind their kings with chains, and their nobles with fetters of iron; ^psalms-149-8

**9** To execute upon them the judgment written: this honour have all his saints. Praise ye the LORD. ^psalms-149-9
