# Psalms 3

**1** Lord, how are they increased that trouble me! many are they that rise up against me. ^psalms-3-1

**2** Many there be which say of my soul, There is no help for him in God. Selah. ^psalms-3-2

**3** But thou, O LORD, art a shield for me; my glory, and the lifter up of mine head. ^psalms-3-3

**4** I cried unto the LORD with my voice, and he heard me out of his holy hill. Selah. ^psalms-3-4

**5** I laid me down and slept; I awaked; for the LORD sustained me. ^psalms-3-5

**6** I will not be afraid of ten thousands of people, that have set themselves against me round about. ^psalms-3-6

**7** Arise, O LORD; save me, O my God: for thou hast smitten all mine enemies upon the cheek bone; thou hast broken the teeth of the ungodly. ^psalms-3-7

**8** Salvation belongeth unto the LORD: thy blessing is upon thy people. Selah. ^psalms-3-8
