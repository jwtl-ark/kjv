# Psalms 20

**1** The LORD hear thee in the day of trouble; the name of the God of Jacob defend thee; ^psalms-20-1

**2** Send thee help from the sanctuary, and strengthen thee out of Zion; ^psalms-20-2

**3** Remember all thy offerings, and accept thy burnt sacrifice; Selah. ^psalms-20-3

**4** Grant thee according to thine own heart, and fulfil all thy counsel. ^psalms-20-4

**5** We will rejoice in thy salvation, and in the name of our God we will set up our banners: the LORD fulfil all thy petitions. ^psalms-20-5

**6** Now know I that the LORD saveth his anointed; he will hear him from his holy heaven with the saving strength of his right hand. ^psalms-20-6

**7** Some trust in chariots, and some in horses: but we will remember the name of the LORD our God. ^psalms-20-7

**8** They are brought down and fallen: but we are risen, and stand upright. ^psalms-20-8

**9** Save, LORD: let the king hear us when we call. ^psalms-20-9
