# Psalms 100

**1** Make a joyful noise unto the LORD, all ye lands. ^psalms-100-1

**2** Serve the LORD with gladness: come before his presence with singing. ^psalms-100-2

**3** Know ye that the LORD he is God: it is he that hath made us, and not we ourselves; we are his people, and the sheep of his pasture. ^psalms-100-3

**4** Enter into his gates with thanksgiving, and into his courts with praise: be thankful unto him, and bless his name. ^psalms-100-4

**5** For the LORD is good; his mercy is everlasting; and his truth endureth to all generations. ^psalms-100-5
