# Psalms 120

**1** In my distress I cried unto the LORD, and he heard me. ^psalms-120-1

**2** Deliver my soul, O LORD, from lying lips, and from a deceitful tongue. ^psalms-120-2

**3** What shall be given unto thee? or what shall be done unto thee, thou false tongue? ^psalms-120-3

**4** Sharp arrows of the mighty, with coals of juniper. ^psalms-120-4

**5** Woe is me, that I sojourn in Mesech, that I dwell in the tents of Kedar! ^psalms-120-5

**6** My soul hath long dwelt with him that hateth peace. ^psalms-120-6

**7** I am for peace: but when I speak, they are for war. ^psalms-120-7
