# Psalms 34

**1** I will bless the LORD at all times: his praise shall continually be in my mouth. ^psalms-34-1

**2** My soul shall make her boast in the LORD: the humble shall hear thereof, and be glad. ^psalms-34-2

**3** O magnify the LORD with me, and let us exalt his name together. ^psalms-34-3

**4** I sought the LORD, and he heard me, and delivered me from all my fears. ^psalms-34-4

**5** They looked unto him, and were lightened: and their faces were not ashamed. ^psalms-34-5

**6** This poor man cried, and the LORD heard him, and saved him out of all his troubles. ^psalms-34-6

**7** The angel of the LORD encampeth round about them that fear him, and delivereth them. ^psalms-34-7

**8** O taste and see that the LORD is good: blessed is the man that trusteth in him. ^psalms-34-8

**9** O fear the LORD, ye his saints: for there is no want to them that fear him. ^psalms-34-9

**10** The young lions do lack, and suffer hunger: but they that seek the LORD shall not want any good thing. ^psalms-34-10

**11** Come, ye children, hearken unto me: I will teach you the fear of the LORD. ^psalms-34-11

**12** What man is he that desireth life, and loveth many days, that he may see good? ^psalms-34-12

**13** Keep thy tongue from evil, and thy lips from speaking guile. ^psalms-34-13

**14** Depart from evil, and do good; seek peace, and pursue it. ^psalms-34-14

**15** The eyes of the LORD are upon the righteous, and his ears are open unto their cry. ^psalms-34-15

**16** The face of the LORD is against them that do evil, to cut off the remembrance of them from the earth. ^psalms-34-16

**17** The righteous cry, and the LORD heareth, and delivereth them out of all their troubles. ^psalms-34-17

**18** The LORD is nigh unto them that are of a broken heart; and saveth such as be of a contrite spirit. ^psalms-34-18

**19** Many are the afflictions of the righteous: but the LORD delivereth him out of them all. ^psalms-34-19

**20** He keepeth all his bones: not one of them is broken. ^psalms-34-20

**21** Evil shall slay the wicked: and they that hate the righteous shall be desolate. ^psalms-34-21

**22** The LORD redeemeth the soul of his servants: and none of them that trust in him shall be desolate. ^psalms-34-22
