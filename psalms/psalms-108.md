# Psalms 108

**1** O God, my heart is fixed; I will sing and give praise, even with my glory. ^psalms-108-1

**2** Awake, psaltery and harp: I myself will awake early. ^psalms-108-2

**3** I will praise thee, O LORD, among the people: and I will sing praises unto thee among the nations. ^psalms-108-3

**4** For thy mercy is great above the heavens: and thy truth reacheth unto the clouds. ^psalms-108-4

**5** Be thou exalted, O God, above the heavens: and thy glory above all the earth; ^psalms-108-5

**6** That thy beloved may be delivered: save with thy right hand, and answer me. ^psalms-108-6

**7** God hath spoken in his holiness; I will rejoice, I will divide Shechem, and mete out the valley of Succoth. ^psalms-108-7

**8** Gilead is mine; Manasseh is mine; Ephraim also is the strength of mine head; Judah is my lawgiver; ^psalms-108-8

**9** Moab is my washpot; over Edom will I cast out my shoe; over Philistia will I triumph. ^psalms-108-9

**10** Who will bring me into the strong city? who will lead me into Edom? ^psalms-108-10

**11** Wilt not thou, O God, who hast cast us off? and wilt not thou, O God, go forth with our hosts? ^psalms-108-11

**12** Give us help from trouble: for vain is the help of man. ^psalms-108-12

**13** Through God we shall do valiantly: for he it is that shall tread down our enemies. ^psalms-108-13
