# Psalms 82

**1** God standeth in the congregation of the mighty; he judgeth among the gods. ^psalms-82-1

**2** How long will ye judge unjustly, and accept the persons of the wicked? Selah. ^psalms-82-2

**3** Defend the poor and fatherless: do justice to the afflicted and needy. ^psalms-82-3

**4** Deliver the poor and needy: rid them out of the hand of the wicked. ^psalms-82-4

**5** They know not, neither will they understand; they walk on in darkness: all the foundations of the earth are out of course. ^psalms-82-5

**6** I have said, Ye are gods; and all of you are children of the most High. ^psalms-82-6

**7** But ye shall die like men, and fall like one of the princes. ^psalms-82-7

**8** Arise, O God, judge the earth: for thou shalt inherit all nations. ^psalms-82-8
