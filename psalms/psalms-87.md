# Psalms 87

**1** His foundation is in the holy mountains. ^psalms-87-1

**2** The LORD loveth the gates of Zion more than all the dwellings of Jacob. ^psalms-87-2

**3** Glorious things are spoken of thee, O city of God. Selah. ^psalms-87-3

**4** I will make mention of Rahab and Babylon to them that know me: behold Philistia, and Tyre, with Ethiopia; this man was born there. ^psalms-87-4

**5** And of Zion it shall be said, This and that man was born in her: and the highest himself shall establish her. ^psalms-87-5

**6** The LORD shall count, when he writeth up the people, that this man was born there. Selah. ^psalms-87-6

**7** As well the singers as the players on instruments shall be there: all my springs are in thee. ^psalms-87-7
