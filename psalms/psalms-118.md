# Psalms 118

**1** O give thanks unto the LORD; for he is good: because his mercy endureth for ever. ^psalms-118-1

**2** Let Israel now say, that his mercy endureth for ever. ^psalms-118-2

**3** Let the house of Aaron now say, that his mercy endureth for ever. ^psalms-118-3

**4** Let them now that fear the LORD say, that his mercy endureth for ever. ^psalms-118-4

**5** I called upon the LORD in distress: the LORD answered me, and set me in a large place. ^psalms-118-5

**6** The LORD is on my side; I will not fear: what can man do unto me? ^psalms-118-6

**7** The LORD taketh my part with them that help me: therefore shall I see my desire upon them that hate me. ^psalms-118-7

**8** It is better to trust in the LORD than to put confidence in man. ^psalms-118-8

**9** It is better to trust in the LORD than to put confidence in princes. ^psalms-118-9

**10** All nations compassed me about: but in the name of the LORD will I destroy them. ^psalms-118-10

**11** They compassed me about; yea, they compassed me about: but in the name of the LORD I will destroy them. ^psalms-118-11

**12** They compassed me about like bees; they are quenched as the fire of thorns: for in the name of the LORD I will destroy them. ^psalms-118-12

**13** Thou hast thrust sore at me that I might fall: but the LORD helped me. ^psalms-118-13

**14** The LORD is my strength and song, and is become my salvation. ^psalms-118-14

**15** The voice of rejoicing and salvation is in the tabernacles of the righteous: the right hand of the LORD doeth valiantly. ^psalms-118-15

**16** The right hand of the LORD is exalted: the right hand of the LORD doeth valiantly. ^psalms-118-16

**17** I shall not die, but live, and declare the works of the LORD. ^psalms-118-17

**18** The LORD hath chastened me sore: but he hath not given me over unto death. ^psalms-118-18

**19** Open to me the gates of righteousness: I will go into them, and I will praise the LORD: ^psalms-118-19

**20** This gate of the LORD, into which the righteous shall enter. ^psalms-118-20

**21** I will praise thee: for thou hast heard me, and art become my salvation. ^psalms-118-21

**22** The stone which the builders refused is become the head stone of the corner. ^psalms-118-22

**23** This is the LORDâ€™s doing; it is marvellous in our eyes. ^psalms-118-23

**24** This is the day which the LORD hath made; we will rejoice and be glad in it. ^psalms-118-24

**25** Save now, I beseech thee, O LORD: O LORD, I beseech thee, send now prosperity. ^psalms-118-25

**26** Blessed be he that cometh in the name of the LORD: we have blessed you out of the house of the LORD. ^psalms-118-26

**27** God is the LORD, which hath shewed us light: bind the sacrifice with cords, even unto the horns of the altar. ^psalms-118-27

**28** Thou art my God, and I will praise thee: thou art my God, I will exalt thee. ^psalms-118-28

**29** O give thanks unto the LORD; for he is good: for his mercy endureth for ever. ^psalms-118-29
