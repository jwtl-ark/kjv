# Psalms 146

**1** Praise ye the LORD. Praise the LORD, O my soul. ^psalms-146-1

**2** While I live will I praise the LORD: I will sing praises unto my God while I have any being. ^psalms-146-2

**3** Put not your trust in princes, nor in the son of man, in whom there is no help. ^psalms-146-3

**4** His breath goeth forth, he returneth to his earth; in that very day his thoughts perish. ^psalms-146-4

**5** Happy is he that hath the God of Jacob for his help, whose hope is in the LORD his God: ^psalms-146-5

**6** Which made heaven, and earth, the sea, and all that therein is: which keepeth truth for ever: ^psalms-146-6

**7** Which executeth judgment for the oppressed: which giveth food to the hungry. The LORD looseth the prisoners: ^psalms-146-7

**8** The LORD openeth the eyes of the blind: the LORD raiseth them that are bowed down: the LORD loveth the righteous: ^psalms-146-8

**9** The LORD preserveth the strangers; he relieveth the fatherless and widow: but the way of the wicked he turneth upside down. ^psalms-146-9

**10** The LORD shall reign for ever, even thy God, O Zion, unto all generations. Praise ye the LORD. ^psalms-146-10
