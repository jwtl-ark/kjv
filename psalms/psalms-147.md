# Psalms 147

**1** Praise ye the LORD: for it is good to sing praises unto our God; for it is pleasant; and praise is comely. ^psalms-147-1

**2** The LORD doth build up Jerusalem: he gathereth together the outcasts of Israel. ^psalms-147-2

**3** He healeth the broken in heart, and bindeth up their wounds. ^psalms-147-3

**4** He telleth the number of the stars; he calleth them all by their names. ^psalms-147-4

**5** Great is our Lord, and of great power: his understanding is infinite. ^psalms-147-5

**6** The LORD lifteth up the meek: he casteth the wicked down to the ground. ^psalms-147-6

**7** Sing unto the LORD with thanksgiving; sing praise upon the harp unto our God: ^psalms-147-7

**8** Who covereth the heaven with clouds, who prepareth rain for the earth, who maketh grass to grow upon the mountains. ^psalms-147-8

**9** He giveth to the beast his food, and to the young ravens which cry. ^psalms-147-9

**10** He delighteth not in the strength of the horse: he taketh not pleasure in the legs of a man. ^psalms-147-10

**11** The LORD taketh pleasure in them that fear him, in those that hope in his mercy. ^psalms-147-11

**12** Praise the LORD, O Jerusalem; praise thy God, O Zion. ^psalms-147-12

**13** For he hath strengthened the bars of thy gates; he hath blessed thy children within thee. ^psalms-147-13

**14** He maketh peace in thy borders, and filleth thee with the finest of the wheat. ^psalms-147-14

**15** He sendeth forth his commandment upon earth: his word runneth very swiftly. ^psalms-147-15

**16** He giveth snow like wool: he scattereth the hoarfrost like ashes. ^psalms-147-16

**17** He casteth forth his ice like morsels: who can stand before his cold? ^psalms-147-17

**18** He sendeth out his word, and melteth them: he causeth his wind to blow, and the waters flow. ^psalms-147-18

**19** He sheweth his word unto Jacob, his statutes and his judgments unto Israel. ^psalms-147-19

**20** He hath not dealt so with any nation: and as for his judgments, they have not known them. Praise ye the LORD. ^psalms-147-20
